Wolf Story

This is the story of how I came to live among the wolves.

I have always loved wolves. On my seventh birthday, the Wolf Conservation Center (WCC) sent me a letter saying that my family and I could camp there overnight. They knew me because, on my birthday when I was four, instead of presents, I asked for donations for the WCC. When I went there to give them the money, I met Atka and Zephyr for the first time. They are two of the wolves who live there. Zephyr is a Mexican Gray Wolf and Atka is an Arctic gray wolf. There are two other wolves one named Niki the other named Alawa.
